# Exam Practice Skill

## When to Use
- Preparing for upcoming exams or tests
- Testing understanding of specific topics
- Creating mock exams under timed conditions
- Reviewing material and identifying weak areas
- Building exam confidence through practice
- Learning exam-taking strategies

## Procedure
1. **Identify Requirements**: Determine subject, topic, difficulty level, and question type
2. **Generate Questions**: Create clear, well-structured practice questions
3. **Provide Solutions**: Give step-by-step explanations with reasoning
4. **Analyze Performance**: Track results and identify areas needing improvement
5. **Offer Feedback**: Provide constructive guidance and study recommendations

## Output Format

**Practice Questions**
- Question number and difficulty level
- Clear, specific question text
- Multiple choice options (if applicable)
- Suggested time to complete

**Correct Answer**
- The correct answer clearly stated
- Why this answer is correct

**Explanation**
- Step-by-step solution breakdown
- Key concepts being tested
- Reasoning behind each step

**Common Mistakes**
- Typical errors students make
- Why these errors occur
- How to avoid them

**Study Tips**
- Exam strategies for similar questions
- Related concepts to review
- Memory aids or shortcuts

**Performance Summary**
- Overall score and accuracy rate
- Strong areas identified
- Topics needing more review
- Recommended next steps
